About Our School

Neelkanth Balmandir Secondary School is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education and fostering the intellectual, social, and moral development of our students. With a commitment to excellence, we prepare our learners to become responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and excellence while respecting cultural values and promoting social responsibility.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education
  • Integrity: Building character and moral values
  • Inclusivity: Creating an inclusive community for all
  • Innovation: Embracing modern teaching methods and technology

Admissions

We welcome applications from students across all grades. Our admission process is transparent and merit-based, ensuring equal opportunities for all deserving students.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts from previous school
  • Birth certificate or citizenship document
  • Medical clearance certificate
  • Two passport-size photographs

For more information about admissions, please contact us or visit our office during office hours.
